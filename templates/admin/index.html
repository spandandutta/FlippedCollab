Flipped Learning Made Easy


The purpose of FlippedCollab is to help students achieve academic success when challenged by a particular teaching methodology called Flipped Learning, through the use of AI / Machine Learning tools and peer collaboration.


Flipped Learning is a methodology, usually used for higher level Honors and AP courses, where students are assigned education videos and presentations to be viewed at home and learn most of the ideas from there.

The main goal of FilppedColab to help students by effectively using their time and by providing smart ways to be prepared for test and quizzes, with the help of AI and Machine Learning Tools. FilppedColab primarily does four things:

  • Generates text transcripts from an educational video using Machine Learning models
  • Summarizes the main ideas from the video for quick review of the content
  • Helps students prepare well for test and quizzes through Question / Answer style interactive learning based on the content from a particular video, whthout having them to wait for watching the entire context over and over
  • Stores the generated transcripts, summarization and helps share among friends and other students so that we can collaborate and reuse
